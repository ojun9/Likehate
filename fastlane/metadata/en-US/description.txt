A simple memo app for keeping track of the things you like and dislike.

Food, places, hobbies, things you are not good with.
You can save your own preferences, or remember the likes and dislikes of someone important to you.

Using it is simple.
Choose “Like” or “Dislike,” then write a short note.

Things you like are “likes.”
Things you dislike are “dislikes.”

Because that is what makes you, you.

Look back at the likes and dislikes you have collected,
and use them as a small reminder of yourself or someone you care about.

A quiet memo app for the things you like and dislike.

Food, places, hobbies, feelings, and things you want to avoid. Save your own preferences, or remember the likes and dislikes of someone important to you.

Add people, choose a preset image or photo, and keep notes in simple Like and Dislike lists.

Compare two people to find what both enjoy, what both avoid, and what makes each person different.

No limits on likes or dislikes. Free users can add up to three people. Lifetime Premium unlocks more people and hides ads.
